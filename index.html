Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Free Birds Club

What is Free Birds Club?

Free Birds Club is a community-driven meme token on the Pump.fun platform that symbolizes freedom in the crypto space. Our project focuses on building a strong community while providing a fun and potentially rewarding investment opportunity.

How can I buy $BIRDS tokens?

You can buy $BIRDS tokens by setting up a compatible wallet (like MetaMask), purchasing ETH or SOL from an exchange, transferring it to your wallet, connecting to Pump.fun, and swapping for $BIRDS. Check our "How to Buy" section for detailed steps.

Is the liquidity locked?

Yes, 80% of the initial liquidity is locked for 6 months to ensure price stability and build trust with our community. Additionally, contract ownership has been renounced, making it impossible for developers to modify the contract in ways that could harm investors.

Are there any transaction fees?

Yes, there is a minimal 1% transaction fee: 0.5% is automatically added to the liquidity pool to ensure sustainable growth, and 0.5% goes to marketing and development efforts to promote and improve the project.

How can I join the community?

You can join our community through our social media channels. We're most active on Telegram and Twitter, but we also have Discord and Medium. Check the "Community" section for links to all our platforms.

What makes Free Birds Club unique?

Free Birds Club stands out with its strong focus on community, fair tokenomics, and the symbolism of freedom. We aim to create not just a token but a movement that empowers individuals in the crypto space. Our community-driven approach and clear roadmap also set us apart from many other meme tokens.
